Homey Hostels App

Get Started

For more than a decade, students at Ghanaian universities like KNUST, University of Ghana (Legon), University of Cape Coast, and others have faced a major challenge in securing reliable accommodation at the start of each academic year. This has often led to students being scammed by fraudulent hostel owners or resorting to commuting long distances every day to attend lectures.

As a solution to the issue, Homey Hostels provides a one-stop platform where students and property owners can securely find and book affordable hostel accommodation. With a user-friendly interface, secure payment gateways, and a vetted system for both renters and property managers, Homey Hostel App ensures a smooth, trustworthy, and efficient hostel booking experience.

Key Features:

  • Secure Hostel Booking: Renters can find verified, available hostels near their universities, with detailed descriptions, pricing, and availability.
  • Vetting System: All property owners and renters are vetted and approved before engaging in any transactions on the platform.
  • Affordable Rates: Find accommodation options that suit your budget, with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
  • Seamless Experience: Whether you're a property owner or a renter, the app makes it easy to manage bookings and communicate securely.
